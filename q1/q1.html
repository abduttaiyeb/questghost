The Day it all Began

-

26 July

My Dumb programming skills as usual being a snob,
An XML error had done a beautiful Job.
The logs I expected to see in my Table,
Ended up being in your DM, pretty Unstable.
Always being a Nerd and temped furious,
I finally found some peace with you for which I was always Curious.
Read your poems and realised the words' killer,
My brain was rotting with codes and you came as my Serotonin dealer.
My mind felt ecstacy meeting a Ghost,
But I better enjoy seeing you get Roast.
